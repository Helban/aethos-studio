The studio

About AETHOS

A brand is a system that behaves, not a logo that sits still. We design the way it moves, responds, and speaks across every surface.

AETHOS is a small senior team. No account layers, no handoff between three vendors who never talk. The person who sets the strategy is in the room when the motion gets designed and when the site gets built.

We work in the open. You see the system taking shape in Figma, moving in a real prototype, and running live before launch. What we hand your engineers is buildable to the pixel, because we build too.

The AI era changed what a brand has to do. It has to hold up inside products that generate, respond, and change in real time. That is the work we came for.
